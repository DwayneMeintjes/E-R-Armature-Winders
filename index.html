About Us

E&R Armature Winders was established in 1996 by Eric Schoombie in Rustenburg. As the company grew, Mr. Schoombie decided to enlist two active shareholders namely: Mr. Mario Schoombie and Mr. Samuel T. Molemane to join forces with his business.

In 2007 construction for our new and improved workshop began. The workshop was completed in December 2011, in January 2012 we officially moved into our new bulding.

We are proud to call this building our own and pride ourselves on the continuous improvement in and around the company and workshop.
