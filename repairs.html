Repairs

Pump Repair Division

We have a dedicated pump repair division within our facility, solely for the purpose of repair and fitment of all pumps.
 
We specialize in the repair of vertical spindle pumps (also known as the Series 300/Series 500).
We repair and build complete new vertical spindle pumps. We are an accredited repairer and distributor of Walter Meano Engineering products.
 
We can also supply and repair all electrical driven pumps
ie. submersible pumps and other vertical spindle pumps.

Rewinding Division

All rewinding is done in-house. We have trained and qualified armature winders.
 
We have a VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnation) tank on the premises.
This process is carried out on all our stators and rotor windings.
 
We use high quality materials for the rewinding of all types of electrical machines (Class H).
We are also accredited by Zest Electric Motors (WEG Group).
 
The complete repair process starts and finishes in our workshop.

Fan Repair Division

Our fan division is equipped with a PLC and Wilson flow grid testing column.
 
We repair standard auxiliary and sound attenuate underground ventilation fans.

Winch Repair Division

Our winch motor repair division is of high standard,
we use quality products and materials on all our winch motors.
 
We also install Earth Path Tester Complete Units (EPT unit) on request.
